OMG, I love this tea!!!
by CustomerXXX on October 12, 2013

This is my first time trying a blooming type tea. I opened the metal canister and found individually packed little balls of tea. I reviewed the instructions and chose a tea to try out. I don't have a glass tea pot but I do have glass bowls. I boiled water and poured it into the glass bowl with the peach flavored tea ball. It was so neat to see it open up or bloom. It opened up and after a few minutes I had peach tea. The tea itself is really good. It tastes like peach with a little tea taste. I've also tried the melon flavor and that one was great too. I am looking to purchase a glass tea pot and saving the others to show my friends because the presentation of the tea blooming is a show in itself. The tea I received has over a year to go until it expires so I have some time. Great stuff.
